Bo Ah Kwon

I’m a product and visual designer with a background in psychology research. I was inspired to pursue a career in design after having helped a marketing professor at the Stanford Graduate School of Business write the book The Dragonfly Effect, which proposed harnessing behavioral science, design thinking, and technology for impact. I’m interested in crafting beautiful, impactful digital experiences while marrying my interests in behavioral science, business, and visual arts.
